The Broken Chain

Welcome to Cornerstone

In this world, the ability to plan, coordinate, and lead others was once a gift shared freely, and then, seven hundred years ago, it was almost entirely lost. What remains is a city that has forgotten how to work together, run by guilds that no longer quite agree on whose job anything is.

A few people still carry the old gift. You are about to find out whether that includes you.

Before your character is arrested and this story properly begins, take some time to walk through Cornerstone as they would have known it: its library, its shrine, its market, its guard post, and the ruin nobody talks about. Nothing you find is required reading. All of it will make the world feel a little more like somewhere you've actually been.
